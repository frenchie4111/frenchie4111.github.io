--- title: Google Cache Javascript Shortcut subject: Automatically takes you to the lastest google cached version of a site tags: Web layout: post --- This is a bookmarklet to automatically load the latest google cache version of a site:

To install, drag Open In Google Webarchive into your bookmarks

Mileage may vary, depending on your browser.

The code:
javascript:document.location=(('http://webcache.googleusercontent.com/search?q=cache:'+window.location))
